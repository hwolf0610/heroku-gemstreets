GEMSTREETS

  • GemStreets has specialised in the sale and letting of commercial properties and retail units in prime city centre locations of Germany.
  • The majority of this research information is now complimentary available as iPhone application called GemStreets to people with a great interest in city centre retail units and commercial properties - such as expansion managers, city managers, urban developers, architects, property landlords and investors.
  • GemStreets provides users anywhere in the world with access to relevant and fully up-to-date information and statistics on about 400 main shopping streets.
  • Furthermore, a map is also provided of retail space occupation in all streets and the position of the individual retail outlets, making GemStreets an interesting option for shopping tourists.
